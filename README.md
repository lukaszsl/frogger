## Rules

* Avoid the bugs - those are your enemies
* You are moved to the starting point if the bug catch you
* Try to reach the water us quick as possible - it finish the game


## How to play

* You can move your character like this:
	- UP ⇧ (up arrow)
	- DOWN ⇩ (down arrow)
	- LEFT ⇦ (left arrow)
	- RIGHT ⇨ (right arrow)
